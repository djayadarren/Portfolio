About

I build dependable systems from requirements through validation, then iterate until the behavior is measurable and testable.

I am currently a Systems Design Engineer Intern at Hitachi Rail.

My role involves drawing track maps in AutoCAD for transportation systems all over the globe including Vancouver, Kuala Lumpur, Istanbul, and Hong Kong. As a part of the signalling design team, I also contributed into analyzing systems performance and failure management of our SelTrac product. This involves optimizing velocity profiles and braking curves to improve runtime and headway performance before delivery (I hope the people using our product in the future appreciate the sheer amount of hours in simulation to allow them to have trains coming into a station every 90 seconds). Some of my other responsibilites include managing Guideway Data Tracking Lists in Excel to validate logical elements against customer specifications, updating Systems Design documents in IBM DOORS, and even developing an AI agent and scripts to optimize and improve our daily workflow. But enough talking about my experience, outside work I'm truly fascinated with Hardware Systems! I love learning/building different computer architecture.
